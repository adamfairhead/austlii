Commonwealth/National funding

  • The Australian Research Council (ARC) has awarded competitive research grants and research infrastructure grants to AustLII since its inception. ARC/DEETYA grants have been AustLII's largest single source of support. N/A $284,000
  • CCH Australia Limited assists with the funding of AustLII's Commonwealth resources. $50,000 $50,000
  • The Legal Practitioners' Liability Committee assists with the funding of AustLII's Commonwealth resources. It considers AustLII to be an invaluable risk management service for its insured solicitors and barristers. N/A $50,000
  • The Australian Taxation Office assists with the funding of the Taxation Law Library. N/A $50,000
